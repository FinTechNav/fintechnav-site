Heavy Pour Wine — Email Assets

CDN base: https://cdn.heavypourwine.com/Email/[AssetName].png

These are the hotlinked assets for the winery onboarding transactional emails (sendOnboardingInvitationEmail and sendOnboardingConfirmationEmail). Unlike the themed icon buckets, everything here is PNG rather than SVG — several major mail clients do not render SVG at all. Filenames are lowercase and hyphenated, which differs from the PascalCase convention in AllModes/, DarkMode/, and LightMode/.

Onboarding Email Icons (168×168, disc baked in)

The pale #f7e9e2 disc behind each glyph is rasterised into the PNG itself, not applied in markup — changing it means regenerating these files. Rendered here at 96px; the emails render them smaller.

Wordmark — theme pair (480×186)

These two are a matched pair, swapped by a prefers-color-scheme picture element — they are not interchangeable. The light-background variant sets “POUR WINE” in near-black and vanishes on a dark background; the dark-background variant sets it in white and vanishes on a light one. Each card below is shown on the background the asset is built for. Rendered at 180px; the emails render at 120px, so the 480px source stays retina-crisp.
