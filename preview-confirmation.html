We received your merchant application for Glassmen Wines. Thanks for taking the time — that’s the big step done.
Here’s what happens next.
Within the next few hours to a few days, watch for a separate email from PayCompass, our payments partner, asking you to review and digitally sign your merchant application. It comes from no-reply@paycompass.com, so keep an eye on your inbox and spam folder — signing it is what moves your application into review.
From there, PayCompass’s underwriting team takes a look. Most applications go through smoothly, but depending on your business they may reach out for a little more:
• Financial documentation — typically the last 3–6 months of business bank statements, and, if your card volume is on the higher side, 3–6 months of statements from your prior processor.
• Identity verification — a soft credit check runs on the business owner to confirm identity and validate your tax ID. It’s a soft inquiry, so it won’t affect your credit score. One heads-up that saves time: if the owner’s credit is frozen with the bureaus, temporarily lift the freeze so the check can go through — if it stays frozen, PayCompass may instead ask for a copy of the owner’s driver’s license to verify identity.
Once your account is approved, PayCompass will also grant you access to a PCI compliance portal — in it, you’ll find the standard security checks every business that accepts cards has to complete. It’s built right into your payment processing, so the questionnaire, the periodic external security scans, your Attestation of Compliance, and a compliance badge for your website all run in one place — no separate service to arrange. This is what the monthly PCI fee in your processing covers, and it’s the same work many wineries pay an outside firm to handle. Because Heavy Pour keeps card data off your own systems, it’s a short, guided self-assessment rather than the long form, renewed once a year.
If anything’s needed from you, PayCompass or your Heavy Pour contact will reach out directly. Otherwise, sit tight — we’ll be in touch about getting you set up.
 
We’re on your side — reach out any time.
