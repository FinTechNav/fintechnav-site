My Life Behind the Drums

When I'm not deep in the world of financial technology, you'll likely find me behind a drum kit. Music has been a constant thread throughout my life, starting with my father's influence as a guitarist.

I actually began my musical journey on guitar, then moved to bass, before finally finding my true home behind the drums. There's something about the rhythm and energy of drumming that provides the perfect counterbalance to the strategic thinking of my day job.

Over the years, I've been fortunate to play with several bands in both Savannah and Atlanta:

In Savannah: I played drums in Capital A↗, a hard rock band. We played many local and regional shows and festivals, released several albums and received regular radio play locally.

In Atlanta: I've kept time for bands like Second Glance↗, Maudlin Ash↗, Campaign↗, and Sonic Speculum↗. The Joy Kills↗ was probably the highlight of my musical journey - we toured nationally, released several albums, built a consistent local following, and even opened for Cage The Elephant at their album release show. Not too shabby!

From First Sip to Wine Passion

In 2006, I was in Las Vegas attending a conference when I had the very fortunate occasion at one of our business dinners to taste a 1982 Chateau Margaux.

Since that night, you could say I have been hooked on fine wine.

What started as appreciation quickly grew into a full-blown passion. In 2025, I even received my WSET Level 2 Award in wines, which is just another step in my formal wine education journey.

My wife Dio and I have gotten involved with the Atlanta High Museum's Wine Auction↗ at the Benefactor level, and we've become investors in wineries in both Tuscany↗ and Napa↗. Along the way, we've traveled the country and the world seeking out amazing wine, food, and people.

For a deeper dive into my wine adventures, check out my dedicated blog at Heavy Pour Wine↗ or follow me on Instagram↗, where I document my ongoing journey into this endlessly enjoyable, educational, and inspirational world.
